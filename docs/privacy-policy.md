# Privacy Policy for ReadZero

**Last Updated: February 5, 2026**

## Introduction

ReadZero ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Saved URLs**: When you share articles or links to ReadZero, we store the URL and extracted content
- **Reading Preferences**: Your app settings and preferences

### Automatically Collected Information
- **Device Information**: Device type and operating system version for app compatibility
- **Usage Data**: How you interact with the app (articles saved, features used)

### Information We Do NOT Collect
- We do not collect your name, email, or personal contact information
- We do not track your location
- We do not access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
- We use anonymous authentication - no account creation required

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Save and organize your reading list
- Extract article content for offline reading
- Generate AI-powered summaries and daily digests
- Improve app functionality and user experience

## AI Processing

ReadZero uses artificial intelligence to:
- Summarize articles you save
- Generate daily digest newsletters
- Extract key points and themes from your reading

**Important**: Your saved content is processed by third-party AI services (Anthropic Claude) to generate summaries. This processing is done securely and content is not used to train AI models.

## Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored securely on Supabase (cloud infrastructure)
- Data is encrypted in transit and at rest
- We implement industry-standard security measures
- Your data is associated with an anonymous user ID, not personal identifiers

## Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:
- **Supabase**: Database and authentication
- **OpenRouter/Anthropic**: AI-powered content analysis
- **Jina AI**: Article content extraction
- **xAI (Grok)**: X/Twitter post extraction

These services have their own privacy policies and data practices.

## Data Retention

- Your saved articles are retained until you delete them
- You can delete individual articles or all data at any time
- Deleted data is permanently removed from our servers

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your saved data within the app
- Delete your data at any time
- Export your reading list (feature planned)
- Opt out of AI-generated summaries

## Children's Privacy

ReadZero is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
